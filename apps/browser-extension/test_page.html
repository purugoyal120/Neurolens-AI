In typical enterprise tools, users often encounter text styled directly with confusing colors, such as Error: Budget Deficit Exceeded or Success: Milestone Phase 1 Done. When NeuroLens is active, it detects these exact phrases, shifts the font colors to high-contrast accessible variants, and automatically inserts inline meaning badges directly adjacent to the text!
